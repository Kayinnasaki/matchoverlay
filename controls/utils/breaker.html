Score and Swap send updates automatically. To change player names or event title you must hit Submit Names. Also use Submit Names to submit a Username.

The controlboard doesn't automatically sync betweens instances to avoid people stepping on each others toes (both pressing +1 on a score at the same time for example).

If Refresh is red, it is recommended to hit that before making your own changes.

User names in the user list will flash when they submit changes, letting you know who is paying attention and how active they are.

Select Tourney ID to get an automatic list of all the participants. You may have to doubleclick the arrow to see all participants in Firefox
